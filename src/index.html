What Are

Hard Money Loans

A hard money loan is a short-term, asset-based loan used primarily by real estate investors to finance property purchases or renovations. These loans differ from a mortgage, because the loan application is based mainly on property value rather than credit score. As a result, these loans will usually have higher interest rates. They are a good resource for those that need quick financing or who can not qualify for a traditional loan.

Pros of Investing in Hard Money Loans

  • High returns: Hard money loans usually have higher returns than stocks, bonds, and mutual funds.
  • Short-term investment: Hard money loans are usually short-term investments. Loan terms range from a few months to a few years. Investors will be able to quickly earn returns on their investment without locking up their money for a long period of time.
